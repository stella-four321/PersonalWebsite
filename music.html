My Music

Coucou

May, 2020

Coucou is an original song I wrote in the Summer 2020. This song is about missing my partner during the quarantine in which long distance is inevitable. "Coucou", which means "hi" in French, is constantly repeated throughout the song due to a long to have a simple small talk or a text saying "hi" from my partner. This song consists of me having a conversation with myself, suffering from confinement and loneliness, longing and missing of my partner. that I miss saying hi my partner
My sister, Celing Fournier, made the animation for my music video. The somehow slow motions of symbolic shapes shows how slowly time flows during confinement and being alone for a long period of time.

Christmas Loneliness

November, 2020

I wrote Christmas Loneliness when Christmas holiday was around the corner. When Christmas slowly approached, I realized that I've never celebrated Christmas with a partner. It was also the time that I lost a special person in my life. I tried to juxtapose the jolyness of Christmas and the vibrantness of street lights with the loneliness I was feeling during that time. I wished I was heard and cared for when I said I reached the shooting star.
