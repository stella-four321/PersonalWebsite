Introduction

Hi, I'm Stella. I am currently a 3rd year majoring in electrical engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology. I fell in love with engineering when I realized that it is a very creative and interdisciplinary subject field. For example, I am currently in a research making artistic robotic painting system. Since I am an artistic and creative person, I love how I can combine the more artistic subject to a more concrete field, like engineering and robotics.

Exploration


One thing that I am constantly doing everyday is to explore. I am a curious person and I am willing to learn any new skills provided to me. For example, I self-learned HTML and Java to build this personal website. Furthermore, I am a Peer Instructor at an interdisciplinary makerspace on campus in which I help students fascilitate their personal projects. To become a Peer Instructor, I get to learn how to operate all the equipment in the makerspace, including PCB manufacturing, 3D printing, laser cutting, wood working, embedded systems, and electronics benchtop.
